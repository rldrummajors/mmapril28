Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2017

Show Name: It is

Group: Carolina Crown

Location: DCI World Finals

Experience Level: DCI

Context: Carolina Crown's 2017 production, "It is," was a thought-provoking show exploring the ideas of existence and identity though music, choreography, and design. Though uncommon in DCI, this show featured a singer who sings "For Good" - a song that is cherished in our music program here at RL. "It is" ultimately earned Crown a 3rd place finish with a score of 96.575.
